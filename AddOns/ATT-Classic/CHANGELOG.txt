1.3.0 Release Notes
Contributions made by Crieve:
* Fixed a display bug for Runeforging spells.
* Added objectIDs for Outland Schools.
* Bejeweled Dagger / Gleaming Scale Breastplate still available for the Horde.
* Added Species ID to the interface options. The BOE T0 pieces now count as collected if they're in your bags waiting to be turned in.
* Fixed the format of Heartswood.
* Fixed a one of the druid swift flight form quest givers.
* Added timelines for a couple of Elywynn Forest rare items.
* Added some missing mapIDs for low level blacksmithing quests.
* The quest "Feedin' Da Goolz" can't be completed if you finished Betrayal.
